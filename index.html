  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    Implemented Online Attendance Management System: This informs parents about their child's/children's activites in the school, instantly. Once a student reaches the school, the parents will be informed about the staus via text messages. This helps the school keep a record of the student's absenteeism. Students can mark the attendance by logging in, and this is stored in a database, which can later be checked by the Admin. The tracker has so much potential that it can identify which students are irregular to their classes and not ready to establish connectivity with the concepts of their curriculum's subjects. As soon as you enter a student's Enrollment Number or Name, details like availability in classes, number of lectures attended will be displayed and no proxies are possible since the tracker is supported with cloud capabilites. Even the security offered can be so robust and powerful that those students that always fool their deans are caught in a shorter span - just after the admin enters the login details into this tracker. All this will improve accuracy and transparency in any of the eductional institutions as students and their parents are pre-informed about the real-time status of leave requests and absenteeism in a confidentail manner at reduced costs.
